  • Pet For Anyone?

Chameleons are not pets for everybody; they take a lot of effort and experience. They are very difficult to maintain and inexperienced reptile owners should not start with this reptile. However, they are also not the most difficult exotic pet to take care of.... Panther Chameleons, Veiled Chameleons, and Jackson's Chameleons are the most widespread species that kept as pets.


If you are willing to commit to having a pet chameleon, the prices could ramge from $75-$100 for a typical veiled chameleon from a pet store to $700-$800 for certain species/varieties/colors, etc.
